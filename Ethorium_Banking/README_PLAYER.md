# Ethorium Banking - Player Guide

Welcome to the new unified Banking System for Ethorium.

## Accounts & IBANs
- Every citizen has a **Personal Account** generated with a unique IBAN (ex: `LS12-ETH-12345678`).
- **Business Accounts** are generated automatically when a business receives its first deposit.

## Bank Cards & PINs
You can generate Bank Cards directly at the Bank NPC or Terminal:
1. **Standard**: $10,000 Daily Limit ($250/mo fee). 
2. **Premium**: $25,000 Daily Limit ($750/mo fee).
3. **Gold**: $75,000 Daily Limit ($2,500/mo fee).
4. **Platin**: UNLIMITED Daily Limit ($10,000/mo fee - Requires $100M balance).

*Warning: Entering an incorrect PIN 3 times will permanently lock your card!*

## Loans & Collaterals
Need a quick loan? Visit the Bank to apply.
- Your credit score determines your limit.
- You must provide **Collateral** (a House or Vehicle you fully own). Rented houses or financed cars do not count.

## ATMs
You can use any ATM around the city. The ATMs are fully interactive. Just insert your card, type your PIN, and withdraw or deposit cash seamlessly. 

## Paying Bills (Invoices & Receipts)
- **Invoices**: Some jobs will send you an open invoice. You must go to a Bank UI to pay it manually.
- **Receipts**: Auto-deducted directly from your account (e.g. buying a soda or filling fuel at a Gas Station). Gas receipt logs contain your vehicle plate for traceability!
